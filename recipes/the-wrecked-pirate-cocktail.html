The wrecked pirate cocktail

Celebrate summer...and pirates...and Captain Jack Sparrow with this refreshing Disney-Inspired Wrecked Pirate Cocktail!
This refreshing summer cocktail is a taste for the tropics. This tropical cocktail is perfect to sip while lounging by the pool.

Ingredients

  • 2 parts spiced rum
  • 1 part coconut rum
  • 2 parts pineapple juice
  • Splash Blue Curacao
  • Splash Lemon Lime Soda
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • Fresh Pineapple garnish
  • Maraschino Cherries garnish

Instructions

  1. Add 1 cup ice to a shaker.
  2. Add the rums, Blue Curacao, pineapple juice and lime juice.
  3. Shake well.
  4. Pour in a glass and add a splash of lemon-lime soda.
  5. Garnish with pineapple and cherries.
