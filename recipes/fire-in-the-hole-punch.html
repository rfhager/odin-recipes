Fire in the hole punch

This Fireball Whiskey Punch Recipe is only THREE INGREDIENTS and is perfect for any party! Delicious, and with that amazing Fireball punch!

Ingredients

  • Fireball Whiskey
  • Apple juice concentrate
  • Sprite

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients together until the concentrate melts.
  2. Optional: Rim the glasses with honey and dip in sugar.
